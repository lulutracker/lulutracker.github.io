lulu tracker is a website, and iOS + Android application that enables lululemon fanatics to track when their favourite items or items they are interested in from the past come in stock online. A notification is sent to your e-mail, iOS/Android application, website account, and the most tracked items will be posted on @lulutracker's instagram and twitter.

currently the website and iOS + Android applications are in development, but will be launched Fall of 2020.

Join the 2500+ other lululemon fanatics on the waitlist!
